Component Library

Component One: SoundCloud - Header

Target Audience

SoundCloud is designed for males and females from 14-45. Anyone that would want to either make music and put it on there for others to listen to or if the user is looking for some new music. The site is free to use and almost resemebles a social media site because the user can comment on someone else work.

Client/User Needs

With this site, SoundCloud is to get users to listen and share pieces of music that the user has found fascinating. On the other hand the user that comes to SoundCloud is looking for something new or something that they have already listen to. The user may also have the intent of finding that new piece o music and sharing so that the users follows/friends get to listen to something they found good. The user could also put music on here and potentally get noticed and move on to better things.

Critque

The dark background on the SoundCloud header makes everything on the header very readable. The way they made the logo stand out the most because of a really bright color on a dark background. The orange logo creates a cheerful enviroment when finding new music that the user may want to listen to. The grey scale text really make the text more readable and also with the buttons being orange it just makes the user want to click on them. The use of the sans-serif font really fits the more modern look and attracts more of the younger age group. The navigation is really easy to use and is set up really well. The footer is off to the side to give the site a feel like the music a user can find is endless.

Component Two: Twitch.tv - CTA

Target Audience

Twitch.tv is designed for mainly males between the ages of 10-40. The user is looking for people who provide entertainment to others. Twitch.tv has numerous games for other user to be entertained by. This site is also for people who support what twitch users do by donating and subscribing to their channel.

Client/User Needs

With this site, Twitch is to get users to be entertained by other users and make money. When a user comes to the site they want to be entertained by the many other users that have a channel and entertain others. The user also may want to help out others so other users will donate. The user may want to messsage a streamer and ask them how they did it so that they can become a streamer as well and entertain others. User may come to the site to obtain friends with similar likes so when they do come on Twtich their friends may be online and they can talk to them while being entertained.

Critque

Twitch.tv uses a white and dark contrast which is very easy to see. It also makes the site more readable. The use of grid of equals makes the site seem more uniform and fit to their purpose of entertaining others. The CTA of the site is in a dark contrast with the video in the middle which is like a feature. This is good because some users may just be looking for some quick entertainment. The sans-serif font gives it a modern look because of the fact that stream in general is new(somewhat) to the internet. Also with the CTA the user doesn't have to go that far if they only want to look at the featured stream because it gives a general description of what the user is streaming or just about the user streaming.
