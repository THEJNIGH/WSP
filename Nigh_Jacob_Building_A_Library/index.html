Component Library

Component One: SoundCloud - Header

Target Audience

SoundCloud is designed for males and females from 14-45. Anyone that would want to either make music and put it on there for others to listen to or if the user is looking for some new music. The site is free to use and almost resemebles a social media site because the user can comment on someone else work.

Client/User Needs

With this site, SoundCloud is to get users to listen and share pieces of music that the user has found fascinating. On the other hand the user that comes to SoundCloud is looking for something new or something that they have already listen to. The user may also have the intent of finding that new piece o music and sharing so that the users follows/friends get to listen to something they found good. The user could also put music on here and potentally get noticed and move on to better things.

Critque

The dark background on the SoundCloud header makes everything on the header very readable. The way they made the logo stand out the most because of a really bright color on a dark background. The orange logo creates a cheerful enviroment when finding new music that the user may want to listen to. The grey scale text really make the text more readable and also with the buttons being orange it just makes the user want to click on them. The use of the sans-serif font really fits the more modern look and attracts more of the younger age group. The navigation is really easy to use and is set up really well. The footer is off to the site to give the site a feel like the music a user can find is endless.
