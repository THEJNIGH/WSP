About YouTube

Launced in May 2005, YouTube allows billions of people to discover, watch and share orginally-created videos. YouTube provides a forum for people to connect, inform, and inspire others across the globe and acts as a distribution platform for orginal content creators and adverstisers large and small.

YouTube is a Google company.
